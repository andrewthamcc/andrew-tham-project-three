WAR

Welcome to war, General! What name should your troops address you as?

General , we're under attack by and their forces from Beyclic Scard! You'll have to fight against their army of cards. You will be sending our forces into battle turn after turn and whoever is mightier will win. In the event of a stalemate you'll have to send three waves of reinforcements and follow it up by a single winner take all showdown. If you do enough damage to the forces from Beyclic Scard we will be victorious!


Good luck, General. We're counting on you...

You will be playing the card game war against a computer opponent with the following rules:

  • Each turn you and the computer will both play a card.
  • Whoever has a higher card (Ace high) will win that turn and collect the played cards into their pile.
  • In the event of a draw, three aditional cards will be played face down followed by one face up card. The player with the higher card collects the pile of all 10 cards.
  • Play will continue until one player wins 70% of the deck (36 cards).
  • At any time, the player can call a ceasefire and make peace. This will result in no winner as there are truly no winners in war.
