Battle Progress:
1. Player clicks battle button
2. Player buttons are disabled so user can't spam controls
3. Cards appear on playing area
4. Win/Lose appear
5. Cards hide
6. Buttons are enabled

Ceasefire Progress:
1. Buttons are disabled
2. Module appears congratulating user on choosing peace
3. Score given 
4. Ask if they'd like to play again

War Happens:
1. Buttons removed
2. War button appears
3. Background changes to camoflauge
4. Cards are dealt
5. Battle cards reveal
6. Win/Lose
7. War button removed
8. Buttons appear

Win/Lose:
1. Congratulate user on efforts (message will differ)
2. Remind that war is actually a terrible thing
3. Ask if they'd like to play again
